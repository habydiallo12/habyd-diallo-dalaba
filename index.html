All about dalaba

Dalaba is a beautiful city in Guinea Conakry in West Africa. Dalaba has astonishing forests with nice weather.It has one of most lovely waterfalls in the world which is called chute de Ditinn.

The people of Dalaba are kind and welcoming towards everyone.
